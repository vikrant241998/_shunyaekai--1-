RIOT RX ONE

RIOT RX ONE is a very low cost entry to robotics, fast, accurate and reliable and easy to program yet capable of the most complex tasks. It has a useful 500mm reach. Designed by our young engineers, building on technology is a breakthrough in bench top robotics. It is a 6 axis articulated robot arm but with a 500mm reach. It is lighter, faster and quieter, using high efficiency digital motors driving through steel reinforced polyurethane timing belts. Optional incremental optical encoders provide exceptional integrity as well as safety.

User Case

RIOT RX ONE from Shunya Ekai Technologies can be applied to various production processes,
from simple to precision tasks, and provide optimized solutions.

Pick & Place

This general solution is applied for transporting parts or products in all manufacturing sites. You can experience the differentiated task performance, the innovative motion algorithm, and the intuitive and simple teaching feature of RIOT RX ONE.

Packaging

This solution is applied to arrange finished products in the transport box or align them in a specific area. It can easily perform packaging and palletizing tasks in a variety of patterns.

Inspection

This solution is applied to the inspection lines in all manufacturing industries from automotive to electronics, general machinery, food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Not only can the cobots perform a visual inspection using the camera system, but they can also perform a general inspection that requires the capability of workers.

Machine Tending

This solution is applied to part production lines in major manufacturing industries. RIOT RX ONE can easily increase the production efficiency for simple processes that handle the finished products from the automation system.

FAQ
What is the RIOT RX ONE?
RIOT RX ONE is a 6 axis articulated robot, designed and manufactured by Shunya Ekai Technologies to solve various automated applications.
What is the payload capacity of RIOT RX ONE?
Payload capacity of RIOT RX ONE is 2Kg.
What is the working envelope of RIOT RX ONE?
The working envelope of RIOT RX ONE is 1000mm.
How can RIOT RX ONE be programmed?
RIOT RX ONE is programmed via teach pendant.
What all applications can RIOT RX ONE be used for ?
RIOT RX ONE can be used for various applications viz: Pick & Place, Packaging, Machine Tending, Inspection, Assembly, Screwing etc.
