    FAQ
    Are these robots only affordable by big businesses?
    We make sure that the Robots are affordable and accessible to all. The Robots are not only helpful in multiple industries but are also utilised by individuals to perform various tasks, for example a lot of domestic operations are highly useful as smart home Robots.
    How is investing in robots better?
    One Robot has multiple features and hence can perform tasks more than one individual can. They perform tasks that are hazardous for humans, improve efficiency and customer satisfaction, and reduce customers time and money expenditure.
    Will I have to instruct the robot every time I want it to perform a task?
    It will depend on the particular Robot however all Autonomous Mobility Robots have machine learning capabilities and can easily comprehend instructions.
    Do the robots need servicing?
    Yes, they require minimal servicing after a certain period of time.
    Are these Robots' privacy protected?
    Yes.
    Is my information being tracked or transmitted to other devices using the robot?
    No, the data is not transmitted without your permission.
